About Decent

Written from inside the experiment.

Something is shifting under the surface of the web. Private servers are becoming thinking machines. Relays are becoming nervous systems. Crews of humans and models are making things that weren’t possible last year. Most of it has no name yet. None of it has a story.

Each piece is one idea, explored from first principles — and a working proof that the idea is real enough to publish on. The format is the argument: that attention is scarce, that long thinking still matters, and that the best way to understand a technology is to use it in public.

This is co-creation, not journalism. Send a paragraph, a diagram, a thesis, a finished piece — any format, any state of finish. We will work it into print. We are also looking for design contributions (typography, layout, illustration, data visualisation) and, when the time comes, printing. The first issue is online. The next one will land on a doorstep.
